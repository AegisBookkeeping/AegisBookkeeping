About Aegis

Your Shield
Against Financial Chaos.

Jake
Founder, Aegis Bookkeeping — Kentucky

My background is in real estate investing — and like most investors, I learned early that the numbers running your business are just as important as the deals you're closing. I watched too many capable business owners lose track of their finances not because they weren't smart, but because no one had set up a system that actually worked for them.

That's why I started Aegis. I'd experienced firsthand what messy books cost — in stress, in bad decisions, and in money left on the table at tax time. I wanted to build a bookkeeping firm that treated small business owners the way they deserved: with transparency, precision, and a real person who actually picks up the phone.

Today, Aegis serves real estate investors, marketing agencies, and coaches across the country — all remotely, all with flat-rate pricing, and zero surprise fees.
