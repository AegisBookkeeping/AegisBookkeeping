About Aegis

Your Shield
Against Financial Chaos.

Jake
Founder, Aegis Bookkeeping — Kentucky

Before building Aegis, Jake served in the U.S. Army — where precision and accountability weren't optional. That same standard carried over into real estate investing, where he learned early that the numbers running your business are just as important as the deals you're closing. He watched too many capable business owners lose track of their finances not because they weren't smart, but because no one had set up a system that actually worked for them.

That's why he started Aegis. He'd experienced firsthand what messy books cost — in stress, in bad decisions, and in money left on the table at tax time. The goal was simple: build a bookkeeping firm that treated small business owners the way they deserved — with transparency, precision, and a real person who actually picks up the phone.

Today, Aegis serves real estate investors, marketing agencies, and coaches across the country — all remotely, all with flat-rate pricing, and zero surprise fees.
